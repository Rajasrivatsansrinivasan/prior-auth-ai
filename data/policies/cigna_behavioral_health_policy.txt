Policy ID: CIGNA-BH-PSYCH-2024
Payer: Cigna Behavioral Health
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policy Title: Behavioral Health - Psychiatric Medication Prior Authorization

================================================================================
ANTIPSYCHOTIC MEDICATIONS - SECOND GENERATION
================================================================================

1. ARIPIPRAZOLE (Abilify) - NDC/CPT J0401
   - Schizophrenia (ICD-10: F20.x):
     * APPROVED: First-line after failure or contraindication to one generic antipsychotic
     * PREFERRED STEP: Must try generic aripiprazole first (same molecule)
   - Bipolar I Disorder (ICD-10: F31.x):
     * APPROVED: Acute manic or mixed episodes
     * APPROVED: Adjunct to lithium or valproate
   - Major Depressive Disorder - Adjunct (ICD-10: F32.x, F33.x):
     * APPROVED: Adjunct therapy after failure of 2 antidepressants
     * REQUIRED: Documentation of 2 adequate antidepressant trials

2. CLOZAPINE (Clozaril) - NDC
   - APPROVED: Treatment-resistant schizophrenia (failure of ≥ 2 antipsychotics)
   - APPROVED: Suicidal behavior in schizophrenia or schizoaffective disorder
   - REQUIRED: Enrollment in REMS program (mandatory)
   - REQUIRED: Baseline ANC ≥ 1500/μL
   - MONITORING: Weekly CBC x 6 months, biweekly x 6 months, then monthly
   - DENIAL: ANC < 1000/μL (absolute contraindication)

3. LONG-ACTING INJECTABLE ANTIPSYCHOTICS (LAI)
   Paliperidone Palmitate (Invega Sustenna) - CPT J2426:
   * APPROVED: Schizophrenia with documented non-adherence to oral antipsychotics
   * APPROVED: Patient preference after discussion of alternatives
   * REQUIRED: Adequate trial of oral antipsychotic (minimum 4 weeks)
   Aripiprazole Monohydrate (Aristada) - CPT J0401:
   * Same criteria as above

================================================================================
STIMULANTS - ADHD (Adult)
================================================================================
AMPHETAMINE SALTS (Adderall XR) - Schedule II
   - Adult ADHD (ICD-10: F90.0, F90.1, F90.2):
     * APPROVED: After trial of methylphenidate-based medication (any formulation)
     * APPROVED: Documented intolerance to methylphenidate
     * REQUIRED: Adult ADHD diagnosis by psychiatrist or licensed psychologist
     * REQUIRED: Structured diagnostic evaluation (not screening tool alone)
   - Quantity Limit: 30-day supply maximum; no early refills
   - Age restriction: Must be ≥ 18 for adult ADHD indication

LISDEXAMFETAMINE (Vyvanse):
   - Step Therapy: Generic amphetamine salts preferred
   - APPROVED: Failure of 2 generic stimulants OR binge eating disorder
   - Binge Eating Disorder (ICD-10: F50.81):
     * APPROVED: Moderate-severe BED diagnosis by mental health professional

================================================================================
MENTAL HEALTH PARITY COMPLIANCE
================================================================================
Per Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act (MHPAEA):
- Behavioral health PA requirements are no more restrictive than medical PA requirements
- Step therapy requirements mirror those applied to comparable medical conditions
- Any denial must cite clinical criteria equivalent to those used for medical/surgical

================================================================================
TELEHEALTH PRESCRIBING
================================================================================
Effective 2024: Controlled substances (Schedule II-IV) prescribed via telehealth
require in-person evaluation within 30 days for PA continuation under Cigna policy.

================================================================================
APPEALS
================================================================================
All psychiatric medication denials: 
- Standard appeal: 30 days
- Expedited appeal: 72 hours for urgent clinical situations
- Peer-to-peer: Available within 72 hours of denial notification
- External review available after internal appeal exhaustion
