Payer: Aetna Health Plans
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policy Title: Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) - Prior Authorization Requirements

================================================================================
CLINICAL INDICATIONS - APPROVED WITH PRIOR AUTHORIZATION
================================================================================

1. LUMBAR SPINE MRI (CPT 72148, 72149, 72158)
   - Indication: Low back pain persisting for ≥ 6 weeks despite conservative treatment
   - Conservative treatment must include: physical therapy (minimum 6 sessions), NSAIDs, and activity modification
   - Documentation required: Treatment log from physical therapist, prescription history
   - APPROVED: Suspected herniated disc with radiculopathy (pain radiating below knee)
   - APPROVED: Cauda equina syndrome symptoms (bowel/bladder dysfunction)
   - APPROVED: Prior spinal surgery evaluation
   - DENIED: Acute low back pain < 6 weeks without red flags
   - DENIED: Routine screening without clinical indication

2. BRAIN MRI (CPT 70551, 70552, 70553)
   - APPROVED: New onset seizures
   - APPROVED: Headaches with neurological deficits
   - APPROVED: Suspected MS with clinical presentation
   - APPROVED: Post-stroke evaluation
   - APPROVED: Cognitive decline evaluation in patients > 60
   - DENIED: Routine headaches without neurological symptoms
   - DENIED: Headaches responsive to OTC medications

3. KNEE MRI (CPT 73721, 73722, 73723)
   - APPROVED: Acute injury with suspected ligament or meniscal tear (mechanism of injury documented)
   - APPROVED: Failure of conservative treatment ≥ 4 weeks for chronic knee pain
   - APPROVED: Pre-surgical planning
   - DENIED: Acute sprain without instability signs within first 2 weeks

================================================================================
DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================
All MRI requests must include:
- ICD-10 diagnosis code(s)
- CPT procedure code
- Clinical notes from ordering physician
- Evidence of failed conservative therapy (if applicable)
- Ordering physician NPI
- Patient demographics and insurance ID

================================================================================
APPEAL PROCESS
================================================================================
Denied requests may be appealed within 60 days. Expedited review available for
urgent clinical situations. Peer-to-peer review available within 72 hours of denial.

================================================================================
POLICY BASIS
================================================================================
This policy is consistent with ACR Appropriateness Criteria, ACP guidelines,
and evidence-based literature as reviewed by Aetna Medical Policy Committee.
