Policy Title: Orthopedic Surgery Prior Authorization Guidelines

================================================================================
TOTAL JOINT REPLACEMENT
================================================================================

1. TOTAL KNEE ARTHROPLASTY (TKA) - CPT 27447
   APPROVAL CRITERIA (ALL must be met):
   a) Diagnosis of moderate to severe osteoarthritis confirmed by X-ray (Kellgren-Lawrence Grade 3-4)
      ICD-10: M17.11, M17.12, M17.31, M17.32
   b) Conservative treatment failure for ≥ 3 months including ALL of:
      - Physical therapy (minimum 8 sessions documented)
      - NSAIDs or analgesics at therapeutic doses
      - Intra-articular corticosteroid injection (at least 1)
   c) Documented functional limitation (unable to perform ADLs)
   d) BMI < 40 OR documented weight management counseling if BMI ≥ 40
   e) Pre-operative cardiac clearance if age > 65 or cardiac history
   
   DENIAL CRITERIA:
   - K-L Grade 1-2 on most recent imaging (within 12 months)
   - Conservative therapy < 3 months
   - Active joint infection
   - Severe peripheral vascular disease
   - BMI > 45 without documented weight loss program enrollment

2. TOTAL HIP ARTHROPLASTY (THA) - CPT 27130
   APPROVAL CRITERIA (ALL must be met):
   a) Severe hip osteoarthritis, avascular necrosis, or failed ORIF
      ICD-10: M16.11, M16.12, M87.051, M87.052
   b) Conservative treatment failure ≥ 3 months
   c) Pain causing significant functional impairment
   d) BMI < 40 OR weight management documentation
   
   APPROVED WITHOUT STEP THERAPY:
   - Displaced femoral neck fracture (CPT 27236)
   - Failed prior hip arthroplasty (revision, CPT 27134)
   - Avascular necrosis Stage III-IV

3. ROTATOR CUFF REPAIR - CPT 29827
   APPROVAL CRITERIA:
   a) MRI-confirmed full-thickness rotator cuff tear
   b) Failed conservative treatment ≥ 6 weeks (PT, injections)
   c) Significant functional limitation
   
   APPROVED WITHOUT CONSERVATIVE THERAPY:
   - Acute traumatic complete tear with mechanism of injury documented
   - Massive tear with pseudoparalysis

================================================================================
BARIATRIC SURGERY (CPT 43644, 43645, 43775)
================================================================================
APPROVAL CRITERIA (ALL must be met):
- BMI ≥ 40 OR BMI ≥ 35 with at least one obesity-related comorbidity
  (T2DM, hypertension, sleep apnea, GERD, osteoarthritis)
- Documented 6-month medically supervised weight loss program
- Psychological evaluation completed within 6 months
- Nutritional evaluation completed
- No untreated substance abuse within 12 months
- No uncontrolled psychiatric condition

================================================================================
IMPLANT AUTHORIZATION
================================================================================
High-cost implants (>$5,000) require separate implant authorization.
Preferred vendor list applies. Non-preferred implants require clinical justification.

================================================================================
POST-SURGICAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================
All approved surgical cases require:
- Notification of surgical date 5 business days prior
- Post-surgical clinical summary within 30 days
- Compliance with post-op PT requirements for continued coverage
