================================================================================
COVERED ONCOLOGY MEDICATIONS - PRIOR AUTHORIZATION REQUIRED
================================================================================

1. PEMBROLIZUMAB (Keytruda) - CPT J2786, J9271
   - NSCLC (Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer):
     * APPROVED: First-line treatment with PD-L1 TPS ≥ 50% (ICD-10: C34.x)
     * APPROVED: First-line combination with carboplatin/pemetrexed regardless of PD-L1
     * APPROVED: Previously treated NSCLC with PD-L1 TPS ≥ 1%
     * REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION: Pathology report, PD-L1 testing result, EGFR/ALK/ROS1 negative status
   - Melanoma (ICD-10: C43.x):
     * APPROVED: Unresectable or metastatic melanoma
     * APPROVED: Adjuvant treatment for Stage IIB-IV resected melanoma
   - DENIED: PD-L1 untested tumors (testing must be completed first)
   - DENIED: Concurrent use with other checkpoint inhibitors without documentation

2. TRASTUZUMAB (Herceptin) - CPT J9355
   - Breast Cancer (ICD-10: C50.x):
     * APPROVED: HER2-positive early breast cancer (adjuvant)
     * APPROVED: HER2-positive metastatic breast cancer
     * REQUIRED: HER2 IHC 3+ OR ISH amplified test result
     * REQUIRED: ECHO/MUGA baseline cardiac function
   - Gastric Cancer (ICD-10: C16.x):
     * APPROVED: HER2-positive gastric or gastroesophageal junction adenocarcinoma
   - DENIED: HER2-negative tumors
   - DENIED: Without baseline cardiac evaluation

3. IMATINIB (Gleevec) - CPT J9999 (NOC)
   - CML (ICD-10: C91.1x):
     * APPROVED: Philadelphia chromosome-positive CML, chronic phase
     * APPROVED: Accelerated or blast phase CML
     * REQUIRED: Cytogenetic confirmation of Ph+ chromosome
   - GIST (ICD-10: C49.A0-C49.A9):
     * APPROVED: KIT (CD117)-positive unresectable or metastatic GIST
     * REQUIRED: Pathology confirming KIT positivity

================================================================================
STEP THERAPY REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================
For most oncology indications, step therapy requirements are WAIVED when:
- Standard of care mandates the requested agent as first-line
- Clinical guidelines (NCCN, ASCO) support the requested treatment
- Prior authorization is requested by board-certified oncologist

================================================================================
QUANTITY LIMITS AND DURATION
================================================================================
- Initial authorization: 6 months for most agents
- Continuation: Re-authorization required at 6-month intervals
- Required at re-auth: Response assessment (imaging, labs), tolerability documentation
- Disease progression on current therapy: New PA required for regimen change
